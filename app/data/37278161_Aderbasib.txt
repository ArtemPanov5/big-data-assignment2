Aderbasib (codenamed INCB7839) is a sheddase inhibitor that may suppress tumor cell proliferation. Acting on multiple receptor classes and subclasses, aderbasib is observed to regulate the tumor necrosis factor of cancer cells. Aderbasib was being developed by Incyte as a potential adjunctive treatment for metastatic breast cancer.
