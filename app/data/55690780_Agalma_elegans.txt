Agalma elegans is a species of siphonophores in the family Agalmatidae. It has been found in a wide variety of locations, including between the Reloncavi Fjord and the Boca del Guafo Passage, in the Chiloe Inland Sea (CIS), Chile. == References == * Development and metamorphosis of the larva of Agalma elegans (Sars)(Siphonophora Physonectae).
