Mohammed Abul Bashar Shaikh (born 25 December 1986) is a Bangladeshi first- class cricketer. He is a right handed batsman and a right arm medium fast bowler. He represented Barisal Division cricket team in National Cricket League from 2004 to 2010. He also represented Dhaka Division cricket team in 2011-12 National Cricket League.
