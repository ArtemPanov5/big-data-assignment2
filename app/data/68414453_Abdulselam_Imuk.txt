Abdulselam İmük (born 10 October 1999) is a Turkish racewalking athlete. İlmük won the bronze medal in the 20 km walk event at the 2020 Balkan Championship held in Ivano-Frankivsk, Ukraine. This result enabled him to participate at the 2020 Summer Olympics. He was also part of the team, which took the silver medal at the same competition.
