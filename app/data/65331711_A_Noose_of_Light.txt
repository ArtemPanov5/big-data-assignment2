A Noose of Light is a novel by Seamus Cullen published in 1986. ==Plot summary== A Noose of Light is a novel in which djinni do evil things to humans. ==Reception== Dave Langford reviewed A Noose of Light for White Dwarf #78, and stated that "the humour and cruelty are faithful to the source: evil- doers are unremittingly punished, while the reward of virtue is often skimpy until after you're dead. Unevenly paced, but an OK read". ==Reviews== *Review by Chris Morgan (1986) in Fantasy Review, April 1986 *Review by Don D'Ammassa (1986) in Science Fiction Chronicle, #84 September 1986 *Review by Mark Greener (1986) in Vector 135 ==References== Category:1986 novels
