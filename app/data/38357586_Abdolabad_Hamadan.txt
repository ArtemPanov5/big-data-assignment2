Abdolabad (, also Romanized as ‘Abdolābād; also known as ‘Abdollāhābād and ‘Abdulābād) is a village in Shirin Su Rural District, Shirin Su District, Kabudarahang County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 435, in 87 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Kabudarahang County
