ALP-IX Regional Internet Exchange (ALP-IX) is an Internet Exchange Point situated in Munich (Germany). It is operated by DE-CIX Management GmbH. In 2011 the ALP-IX was renamed to DE-CIX Munich to be in line with their main brand.
