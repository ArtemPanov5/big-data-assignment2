Aethes nefandana is a species of moth of the family Tortricidae. It was described by Kennel in 1899. It is found in Austria, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Croatia, Albania, Bulgaria, Romania, North Macedonia, Greece, Russia, Asia Minor and western Kazakhstan.Aethes at funet The wingspan is .
