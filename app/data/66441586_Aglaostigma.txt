Aglaostigma is a genus of sawflies belonging to the family Tenthredinidae. The genus was described in 1882 by Kirby. The species of this genus are found in Europe and Northern America. Species: * Aglaostigma aucupariae (Klug, 1817) * Aglaostigma fulvipes (Scopoli, 1763) * Aglaostigma langei * Aglaostigma nebulosum ==References== Category:Tenthredinidae
