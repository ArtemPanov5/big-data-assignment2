ATN Sony Aath is a Canadian exempt Category B Bengali language specialty channel owned by Asian Television Network (ATN). It broadcasts programming from Sony Aath and Canadian content. Sony Aath is a premium Bengali language movie channel from India. It has the largest Bengali film library in India, with over 1000 titles in its lineup.
