Adrián Johnny Jusino Cerruto (born 9 July 1992) is a professional footballer who plays as a defender for Bolivian club The Strongest. Born in the United States, he represents the Bolivia national team. With Bolivia he has earned 24 caps and has played at 1 Copa America. ==Club career== Jusino played a year of college soccer at Richland College in 2013, before playing for Unión Maestranza and Always Ready in Bolivia, as well as a short spell with Premier Development League side Ventura County Fusion in 2016.
