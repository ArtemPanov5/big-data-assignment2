Aadyam Jolie Pinne Kalyanam is a 2022 Indian Malayalam-language mini-series created by and starring Vimal Kumar and Jisma Jiji, along with Sharan S, Samarth Ambujakshan, Arya Jagadeesh and Smruthi Anish in supporting roles. The series lasted for 4 episodes.
