Achomawi traditional narratives include myths, legends, tales, and oral histories preserved by the Achomawi people of the Pit River basin of Northeastern California. Achomawi oral literature reflects the group's position at the junction of cultural influences from central California, the Great Basin, the Plateau, and the Northwest Coast regions of aboriginal North America. ==See also== * Traditional narratives (Native California) ==Achomawi narratives== * Angulo, Jaime de, and Lucy S.
