Aggression and Violent Behavior is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the study of violent behavior. It was established in 1996 and is published by Elsevier. The editor-in-chief is Izabela Zych (Universidad de Córdoba). == Abstracting and indexing == The journal is abstracted and indexed in: * Current Contents/Social & Behavioral Sciences * Social Sciences Citation Index * Sociological Abstracts * Scopus According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2021 impact factor of 4.874. ==References== ==External links== * Category:Criminology journals Category:Elsevier academic journals Category:Bimonthly journals Category:Academic journals established in 1996 Category:English-language journals Category:Violence journals
