A. K. M. Rezaul Karim Tansen (born 7 February 1953) is a Bangladeshi politician and Jatiya Sangsad member representing the Bogra-4 constituency. He belongs to the Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal party. ==Early life== Tansen was born on 7 February 1953. He has a B.A. degree. ==Career== Tansen was elected to Parliament on 5 January 2014 from Bogra-4 as a Bangladesh Awami League candidate.
