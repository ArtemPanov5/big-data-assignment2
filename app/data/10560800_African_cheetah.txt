African cheetah refers to any of the following cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) subspecies native to Africa: * Southeast African cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus jubatus) * Northeast African cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus soemmeringii) * Northwest African cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus hecki) Category:Acinonyx Cheetah Category:Mammals of Africa
