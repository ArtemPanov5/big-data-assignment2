Abhi vs the Universe (sometimes abbreviated as AVU) is the third studio album by Indian rapper, singer, and songwriter Abhi the Nomad, released on September 12, 2021 by UnderCurrent and Riptide Music. ==Background== Abhi vs the Universe is Abhi the Nomad's first album to be released through UnderCurrent Entertainment, after he signed a deal with the label in 2020. A limited-time cassette tape of the album was produced in a small quantity and sold on Abhi the Nomad's Bandcamp page. In December 2021, nearly three months after the release of AVU, a deluxe edition of the album was released on all platforms. The album spawned a twenty-stop tour entitled Abhi the Nomad: Universe Tour. The tour ran for just over two months from 18 September 2022, to 20 November 2022, and saw support from singer-songwriter and TikTok inflencer Charlie Curtis-Beard. The album was released to mainly positive reviews.
