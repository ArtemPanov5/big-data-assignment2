Acheilognathus deignani is a species of freshwater ray-finned fish in the genus Acheilognathus. It is endemic to northern Vietnam in the Mekong River. It grows to a maximum length of 5.2 cm. Named in honor of Herbert Girton Deignan (1906-1968), Associate Curator of Birds, U.S. National Museum, who collected the type specimen. ==References== Category:Acheilognathus Category:Taxa named by Hugh McCormick Smith Category:Fish described in 1945 Category:Fish of Vietnam Category:Endemic fauna of Vietnam
