150px|thumbnail|right|Former logo. A.C. Juvenes/Dogana is a Sammarinese football club based in Dogana, in the civil parish of Serravalle. The club was founded in 2000 after the merger of S.S. Juvenes (named after a historical side in San Marino) and G.S. Dogana. Until season 2006/07 Juvenes/Dogana was the only team to play in both Sammarinese and Italian leagues, taking part in the Girone A of Campionato Sammarinese and in the Italian amateur levels, but it has since retired from the Italian leagues.Emilia Romagna Amateur football yearbook 2007/08 The team's colors are light blue, red and white. == Honours == *Coppa Titano: 9 ::S.S.
