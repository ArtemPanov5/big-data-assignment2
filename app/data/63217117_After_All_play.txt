After All is a 1929 play by the British writer John Van Druten. After a West End run at the Apollo Theatre it transferred to Broadway in 1931. ==Film adaptation== In 1932 it was adapted into an American film New Morals for Old by MGM starring Robert Young and Lewis Stone.Goble p. 472 ==References== ==Bibliography== * Goble, Alan.
