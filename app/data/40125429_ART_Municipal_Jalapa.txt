ART Municipal Jalapa is a Nicaraguan football team playing at the top level. It is based in Jalapa. They currently play in the Primera División, playing their home matches at the 1,000 Estadio Alejandro Ramos. ==History== After the demise of Deportivo Jalapa in 2007,Talento local – La Prensa a new club was formed in 2011 naming themselves Municipal Jalapa ART. The club won both the 2012 Apertura and 2013 Clausura title to earn promotion to the first divisionNicaragua 2012/13 – RSSSF for the first time in their history and the first time a team from Jalapa appeared in the Primera division in six years.
