Abner Warren Biberman (April 1, 1909 - June 20, 1977) was an American actor, director, and screenwriter. == Early years == Biberman was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, later moving to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He gained early acting experience as a student at the Tome School for Boys prep school. He also attended the University of Pennsylvania. == Career == He was sometimes credited under the pseudonym Joel Judge. == Death == Biberman died at his home in San Diego, California. His obituary in The New York Times gave his age as 69.
