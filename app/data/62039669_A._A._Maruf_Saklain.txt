A. A. Maruf Saklain (1944 - 2017) was a Bangladesh Awami League politician, Bangladesh Army officer, and a former Member of Parliament of Nilphamari-4. ==Career== Saklain retired from Bangladesh Army as a colonel and was a member of Retired Armed Forces Officer's Welfare Association. He was elected to parliament from Nilphamari-4 as a Bangladesh Awami League candidate in 2008. ==Death== Saklain died on 21 April 2017. ==References== Category:1944 births Category:Awami League politicians Category:2017 deaths Category:9th Jatiya Sangsad members
