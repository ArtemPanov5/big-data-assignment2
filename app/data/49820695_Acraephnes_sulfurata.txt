Acraephnes sulfurata is a moth in the family Depressariidae. It was described by Edward Meyrick in 1907. It is found in Australia, where it has been recorded from South Australia and Western Australia.Acraephnes at Markku Savela's Lepidoptera and Some Other Life Forms. The wingspan is about 18 mm for males and 27 mm for females.
