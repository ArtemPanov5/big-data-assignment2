Abardeh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Abardeh-e Soflá; also known as Abar Deh-e Pā’īn and Abardeh-ye Pā’īn) is a village in Shandiz Rural District, Shandiz District, Torqabeh and Shandiz County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran.
