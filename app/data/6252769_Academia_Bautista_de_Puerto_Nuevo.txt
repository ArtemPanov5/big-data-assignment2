Academia Bautista de Puerto Nuevo or ABPN is a private Baptist Academy located in Puerto Nuevo, a part of San Juan, Puerto Rico. The school currently serves 1,100 students in grades from pre-kinder to 12. The academy is sponsored by the Primera Iglesia Bautista de Puerto Nuevo as a service to the community in general. The academy is accredited by The General Council of Puerto Rican Education and the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools.
