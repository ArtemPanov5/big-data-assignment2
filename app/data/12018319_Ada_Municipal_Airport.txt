Ada Municipal Airport is two miles north of Ada, in Pontotoc County, Oklahoma. It is owned by the City of Ada, which is southeast of Oklahoma City. Most U.S. airports use the same three-letter location identifier for the FAA and IATA, but Ada Municipal Airport is ADH to the FAA and ADT to the IATAGreat Circle Mapper: ADT / KADH - Ada, Oklahoma (Ada Municipal Airport) (which assigned ADH to Aldan Airport in Aldan, RussiaGreat Circle Mapper: ADH / UEEA - Aldan, Russia (Aldan Airport)).
