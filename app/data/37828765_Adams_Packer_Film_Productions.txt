Adams Packer Film Productions Pty Ltd. was a short lived film production company. It was owned by Phillip Adams and Kerry Packer.Phillip Adams, "The king and his queen", The Australian 30 April 2011 accessed 4 December 2012 Their first film was We of the Never Never. The company joined the film group Bancour. Packer withdrew from film production afterwards.
