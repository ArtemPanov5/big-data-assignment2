Aaron John Rossi (born December 6, 1980) is an American drummer, most notable for his performance with industrial metal band Ministry and heavy metal band Prong. He was nominated for a Grammy at the 52nd Grammy Awards in 2010. ==Discography== John 5 * Songs for Sanity (2005) Ankla * Steep Trails (2006) Prong * Power of the Damager (2007) * Power of the Damn Mixxxer (2009) Ministry * Adios...
