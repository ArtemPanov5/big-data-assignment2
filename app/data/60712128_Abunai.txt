Abunai (阿布奈; 1635 – May 5, 1675), known as Prince Chahar of the First Rank (察哈尔亲王), was a Mongol prince of clan Borjigin, second son of Ligden Khan.Genghis Khan: A Biography by Zofia Stone He opposed Qing influence in his domain and was subsequently placed into house arrest in Shenyang and his son given his title.
