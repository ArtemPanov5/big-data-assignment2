Abdul Kahir Chowdhury is a Bangladeshi politician. He was elected a Member of Parliament for the Bangladesh Nationalist Party in Sylhet-5 (Kanaighat- Zakiganj) constituency in the February 1996 Bangladeshi general election. He is currently the president of Sylhet District BNP.
