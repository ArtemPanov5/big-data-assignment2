Adeloceras is a genus of Mississippian to Lower Permian nautiloid cephalopods included in the nautilid superfamily Aipocerataceae along with genera like Aipoceras, Asymptoceras, and Solenochilus. ==References== * The Paleobiology Database Aipocerataceae entry * List of cephalopod genera, J.J.
