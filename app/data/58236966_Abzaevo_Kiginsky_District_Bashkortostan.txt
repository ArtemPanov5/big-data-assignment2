Abzaevo (, , Abzay) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative centre of Abzaevsky Selsoviet, Kiginsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 558 as of 2010. There are 6 streets. == Geography == Abzaevo is located 34 km north of Verkhniye Kigi (the district's administrative centre) by road.
