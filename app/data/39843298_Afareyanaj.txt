Afareyanaj (, also Romanized as Āfareyānaj, Āfarīānaj, and Āfaryānaj; also known as Āfarīānach, Āferyānech, and Ruferīāneh) is a village in Ab Barik Rural District, in the Central District of Sonqor County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 106, in 32 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Sonqor County
