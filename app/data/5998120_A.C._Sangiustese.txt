Associazione Calcio Sangiustese is an Italian association football club located in Monte San Giusto, Marche. It plays in Serie D. == History == It was founded in 1956 and played in 2008–09 Lega Pro Seconda Divisione, after having been crowned as Serie D/F winners in 2007–08. In July 2010, because the club did not fulfill the financial requirement, it was expelled from professional league and restarted from Eccellenza.
