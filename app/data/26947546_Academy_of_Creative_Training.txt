The Academy of Creative Training is an independent drama school in Brighton, East Sussex, England. ACT has two sites: one in Kemptown, Brighton and one in Hove. == History == ACT was founded by actor John Moulder-Brown in 1997The Argus along with a group of producers, directors, writers and agents. Classes are in the evenings and at weekends so that students can continue to meet their working and/or domestic commitments whilst training. The school awards a two-year diploma in Acting with a Trinity College London ATCL qualification and a one-year Foundation in Acting.
