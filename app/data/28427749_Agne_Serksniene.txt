Agnė Šerkšnienė (Orlauskaitė) (born 18 February 1988) is a Lithuanian sprinter, who specializes in the 400m. In July 2018 at La Chaux-de-Fonds she ran her personal best and a national record of 50.99. She has also set national records in 200m 22.99 at Zofingen in July 2018 and 400m indoors 52.33 at Glasgow in March 2019. ==Achievements== Representing 2005 EYOF Lignano, 5 55.66 2007 European U20 Championships Hengelo, 6 54.00 2008 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Kaunas, 1 53.56 2009 European Indoor Championships Torino, 6 3:43.42 2009 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Kaunas, 1 54.17 2009 European U23 Championships Kaunas, 5 52.81 2009 European U23 Championships Kaunas, 9 3:40.69 2011 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Kaunas, 1 53.92 2011 Universiade Shenzhen, 17 54.51 2012 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Kaunas, 1 52.56 2012 European Championships Helsinki, 14 53.07 2013 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Šiauliai, 1 52.63 2013 World Championships Moscow, 23 52.48 2014 European Championships Zurich, 9 52.32 2014 European Championships Zurich, 11 3:36.25 2018 World Athletics Indoor Championships Birmingham, 7 52.62 2018 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Palanga, 1 52.03 2018 European Championships Berlin, 6 51.42 2019 European Indoor Championships Glasgow, 4 52.40 2020 Lithuanian Athletics Championships Palanga, 1 52.29 ==References== * Category:1988 births Category:Living people Category:Lithuanian female sprinters Category:Sportspeople from Kaunas Category:Competitors at the 2011 Summer Universiade Category:Athletes (track and field) at the 2020 Summer Olympics Category:Olympic athletes for Lithuania Category:Olympic female sprinters
