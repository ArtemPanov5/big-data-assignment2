Admir Džubur (13 June 1964 – 23 November 2020) was a Bosnian businessman and football administrator. He was president of Bosnian Premier League club Željezničar from 29 June 2019 until his death on 23 November 2020, from complications caused by COVID-19, amid the COVID-19 pandemic in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
