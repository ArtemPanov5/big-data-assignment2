Adán Nigaglioni Loyola (12 January 1930 – 18 May 2016) was a Puerto Rican doctor and educator.Notice of death of Dr Adán Nigaglioni Loyola; accessed June 2, 2016. Dialogando con el doctor Adán Nigaglioni, Rector Emeritus ; accessed June 2, 2016. He graduated from the University of Puerto Rico School of Medicine in 1954 where he was top of his class. He did his internship at the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania. He was a member of Phi Sigma Alpha fraternity. He was the first Puerto Rican and first alumni to be dean of the University of Puerto Rico School of Medicine from 1963 to 1967.
