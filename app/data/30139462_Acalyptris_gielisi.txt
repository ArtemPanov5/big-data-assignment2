Acalyptris gielisi is a moth of the family Nepticulidae. It was described by Van Nieukerken in 2010. It is known from the United Arab Emirates.Nepticulidae and Opostegidae of the world The wingspan is 4.2 mm for males and 4.5 mm for females.
