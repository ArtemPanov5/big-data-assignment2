Abezhdan-e Malmulil (, also Romanized as Ābezhdān-e Malmūlīl; also known as Ābezhdān-e Malmūlīn) is a village in Abezhdan Rural District, Abezhdan District, Andika County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 177, in 34 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Andika County
