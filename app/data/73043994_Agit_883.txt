thumb|Logo of Agit 883 Agit 883 56 51, later shortened to Agit 883, was an anarchist newspaper in the left-wing West Berlin scene. It ran from February 1969 to February 1972. The number in the title was the telephone number of the editorial office, which was co-editor Dirk Schneider's flat at Uhlandstraße 52 in Berlin-Wilmersdorf.
