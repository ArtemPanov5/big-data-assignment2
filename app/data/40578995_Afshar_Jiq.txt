Afshar Jiq (, also Romanized as Afshār Jīq; also known as Afshār Ḩaq, Afshār Jeq, and Ushadik) is a village in Aliabad Rural District, in the Central District of Hashtrud County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 459, in 86 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Hashtrud County
