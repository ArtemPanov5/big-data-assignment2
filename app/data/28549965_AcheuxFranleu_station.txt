Acheux—Franleu station (French: Gare d'Archeux—Franleu) is a former railway station in Chépy and near Acheux-en-Vimeu and Franleu, Hauts-de-France, France. The station opened in 1873 and is located on the Abbeville-Eu railway. The station was served by TER (local) services between Abbeville and Le Tréport operated by SNCF.
