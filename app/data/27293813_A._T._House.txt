The A. T. House is a historic house located at 435 Main Street, Oak Hill in Greene County, New York. == Description and history == It was built in three phases: about 1790, about 1795 through 1815, and about 1830. It is a -story and is one of the oldest structures at Oak Hill. Traditional Dutch construction methods were used in the building.
