Abarema alexandri is a species of plant of the genus Abarema in the family Fabaceae.Barneby & Grimes (1996), ILDIS (2005) It is endemic to Jamaica, where it can be found in woodland or thicket on limestone soils. ==References== * (1996): Silk Tree, Guanacaste, Monkey's Earring: A generic system for the synandrous Mimosaceae of the Americas.
