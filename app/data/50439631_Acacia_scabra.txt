Acacia scabra is a shrub of the genus Acacia and the subgenus Phyllodineae. It is native to Western Australia. A. scabra is thought to be a variant of Acacia nodiflora that has slightly larger phyllodes. ==See also== * List of Acacia species ==References== scabra Category:Acacias of Western Australia Category:Taxa named by George Bentham Category:Plants described in 1855
