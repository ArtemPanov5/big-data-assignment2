The Abdullah restaurant bombing was a terrorist attack that was committed during the Second Iraq War on 11 December 2008. The attack involved a suicide bomber who blew himself up in a crowded restaurant outside of Kirkuk in Northern Iraq. The attack killed at least 55 people.
