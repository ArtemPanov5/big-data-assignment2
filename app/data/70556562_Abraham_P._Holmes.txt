Abraham P. Holmes was a state legislator in South Carolina. He represented Colleton County in the South Carolina House of Representatives from 1870 to 1874.Freedom's Lawmakers by Eric Foner Louisiana State University Press 1996 page 107 He was described as "mulatto".
