Ahmed Adnan Saygun Sanat Merkezi is a station on the Konak Tram line in İzmir, Turkey. Originally named Güzelyalı, it is located along Mustafa Kemal Coastal Boulevard in west Konak. The station consists of two side platforms, one on each side of the boulevard. The station opened on 24 March 2018. ==Connections== ESHOT operates city bus service on Mithatpaşa Street.
