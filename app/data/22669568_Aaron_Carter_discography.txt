American singer-songwriter Aaron Carter released six studio albums (including one posthumously), three extended plays (EP), three compilation albums, five video albums and forty four singles (including five as a featured artist). In 2000 Aaron signed with Jive Records and released three more studio albums; the first of them, Aaron's Party (Come Get It), was his most successful album, and sold more than three million copies in United States alone, being certified 3× Platinum by RIAA.
