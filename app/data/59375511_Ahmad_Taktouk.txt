Ahmad Walid Taktouk (, ; born 29 September 1984) is a Lebanese footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for club Sagesse. == Club career == Taktouk started his career at Akhaa Ahli Aley, before moving to Nejmeh in 2014. After playing as the first goalkeeper for three years, he moved to Bekaa on loan in 2017, before joining Safa the following year. After having returned Nejmeh in 2019, Taktouk was sent on a one-year loan to Shabab Bourj on 21 August 2021. He joined Sagesse in August 2022 on a free transfer. == International career == Taktouk made his international debut on 19 February 2014, in a 3–1 home win against Pakistan.
