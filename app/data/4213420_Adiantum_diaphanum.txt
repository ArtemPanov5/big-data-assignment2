Adiantum diaphanum, the filmy maidenhair fern, is a species of fern in the genus Adiantum, native to East Asia and Australasia, from southern Japan south to New Zealand. It grows to 20 cm long at the most, with very dark green fronds covered with bristles. ==Distribution== It is native to China (Fujian, Guangdong, Hainan, Jiangxi), Taiwan, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam, Australia, New Zealand and the Pacific islands. ==References== diaphanum Category:Ferns of Asia Category:Flora of China Category:Flora of Malesia Category:Flora of Papuasia Category:Flora of the Pacific Category:Plants described in 1828 Category:Taxa named by Carl Ludwig Blume
